# GvG Website Design Ideas

## Response 1 — Technical Precision
<response>
<probability>0.07</probability>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Brutalist Data Terminal

**Core Principles:**
- Monospaced type dominates every surface, reinforcing the "machine intelligence" brand voice
- Hard horizontal rules and numbered sections create a report-like document aesthetic
- Negative space is generous but structured — every gap is intentional, not accidental
- Color is purely functional: Electric Blue marks every interactive or accent element

**Color Philosophy:**
Deep Navy (#1A1A2E) as the primary canvas. Charcoal Dark (#2D2D2D) for secondary panels. Electric Blue (#2979FF) for all CTAs, links, and section markers. Pure White (#FFFFFF) for primary body text. The palette communicates authority and precision — no gradients, no decorative color.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Asymmetric editorial grid. The hero splits into a left-heavy text column (60%) and a right stat column (40%). Sections alternate between full-bleed dark and off-white panels. Pricing cards use a horizontal table-like row layout rather than the typical vertical card stack.

**Signature Elements:**
- Section numbers rendered large (01, 02, 03) in Space Mono at 10% opacity as watermark backgrounds
- Thin 1px Electric Blue horizontal rules separating every major section
- Tags/labels rendered as monospaced uppercase with a left-border accent in Electric Blue

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Hover states reveal Electric Blue underlines on links. CTA buttons have a sharp 0-radius corner. Scroll-triggered fade-in for stat numbers. No playful animations — every motion is purposeful and minimal.

**Animation:**
Subtle fade-up on section entry (0.3s ease-out). Stats count up on scroll into view. No parallax, no heavy transitions.

**Typography System:**
- Display/H1/H2: Space Mono Bold — creates the technical, monospaced authority
- H3/Card titles: IBM Plex Sans SemiBold — readable at smaller sizes
- Body: IBM Plex Sans Regular — clean, humanist, readable
- Labels/Captions: IBM Plex Mono Regular — reinforces the data-terminal aesthetic
</text>
</response>

## Response 2 — Dark Editorial Intelligence (CHOSEN)
<response>
<probability>0.08</probability>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Dark Editorial Intelligence — a fusion of financial data terminal and editorial magazine

**Core Principles:**
- Deep navy backgrounds create an authoritative, premium dark environment
- Space Mono headings give every section a technical, precision-engineered feel
- IBM Plex Sans body text ensures readability at scale without sacrificing personality
- Electric Blue is the single accent color — used sparingly but with maximum impact

**Color Philosophy:**
The site lives in the dark. Deep Navy (#1A1A2E) anchors the hero and alternating sections. Charcoal Dark (#2D2D2D) provides subtle panel differentiation. Electric Blue (#2979FF) appears only on CTAs, links, section labels, and key stat highlights. Off White (#F5F5F5) is used for light-mode alternating sections to create breathing room. The contrast between dark and off-white sections creates a rhythmic, editorial pace.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Left-anchored editorial structure. The hero is a full-bleed dark section with the headline left-aligned and oversized. A persistent left-border accent in Electric Blue marks section labels. The services section uses a numbered list with large ordinals. Pricing uses a three-column card layout with the middle card elevated. The about section is a two-column split with a portrait on the right.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large ghost numbers (01, 02, 03) in Space Mono at 8% opacity behind section content
- Electric Blue left-border on all section label tags (e.g., "THE PROBLEM I SOLVE")
- Monospaced stat blocks with oversized numbers and small-caps labels

**Interaction Philosophy:**
CTA buttons are solid Electric Blue with white text, sharp corners (border-radius: 0). On hover, they shift to Light Blue (#5B9BFF). Navigation links have a subtle Electric Blue underline on hover. Cards lift slightly on hover with a faint blue border glow.

**Animation:**
Entrance animations: fade-up with 40px translate (0.5s ease-out, staggered by 0.1s per element). Stats animate count-up on scroll. Section labels slide in from the left. No looping animations.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Mono 700, 64px — hero headline
- H1: Space Mono 700, 48px — page/section titles
- H2: Space Mono 700, 36px — sub-section headings
- H3: IBM Plex Sans 600, 24px — card titles
- Body Large: IBM Plex Sans 400, 18px — lead paragraphs
- Body: IBM Plex Sans 400, 16px — general copy
- Labels: IBM Plex Mono 400, 12px uppercase — section tags, captions
</text>
</response>

## Response 3 — Stark Contrast Manifesto
<response>
<probability>0.05</probability>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets Data Journalism

**Core Principles:**
- Maximum contrast: pure black sections adjacent to pure white sections
- Typography is the only decoration — no icons, no illustrations
- Grid is rigid and mathematical — 12-column, 8px baseline
- Every design decision can be justified by function

**Color Philosophy:**
Alternating black (#000000) and white (#FFFFFF) sections with Electric Blue (#2979FF) as the only accent. No gradients. No mid-tones except for body text gray (#666666). The starkness communicates uncompromising honesty — the brand's core value.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Strict typographic grid. Hero is full-bleed black with white type. Problem section is white with black type. Services are a three-column grid on white. Pricing is a full-bleed black section. The visual rhythm is created entirely by alternating the background color.

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized pull quotes in Space Mono that break the grid
- Electric Blue used only for the single most important CTA on each section
- Section numbers as full-height watermarks in the background

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Hover states invert colors (black becomes white, white becomes black). Links are underlined by default. No subtle effects — everything is bold and declarative.

**Animation:**
Minimal. Only fade-in on scroll. No movement, no parallax. The design is static by philosophy.

**Typography System:**
- All headings: Space Mono Bold
- Body: IBM Plex Sans Regular
- No font mixing within a section — either all mono or all sans
</text>
</response>

## CHOSEN APPROACH: Dark Editorial Intelligence (Response 2)

The Dark Editorial Intelligence approach best embodies the GvG brand guidelines. It uses the prescribed Deep Navy and Charcoal Dark backgrounds, Electric Blue as the sole accent, Space Mono for all headings, and IBM Plex Sans for body copy. The editorial structure — left-anchored, numbered sections, ghost ordinals — reinforces the brand's analytical and authoritative personality without resorting to decorative elements. The alternating dark/light section rhythm creates visual breathing room while maintaining the premium, data-terminal aesthetic.
